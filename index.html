My Purpose

The world we're living in today is the product of all our past decision. Going with that same logic, all our decisions today will shape our future. And that is why making the best, most rational, empathetic, and sustainable decision is significant for designing the world we all want to live in. But we know that human is not the best decision-maker on this planet, and that's where statistics and data can help. My dream is to contribute in creating a data-driven society where everyone can makes informed decision that is based on facts or the currently available data. That's why I am dedicating my life to build a robust data-driven decision-making tools and empowering others to be more thoughtful of their decision.

My Values

Out of all the essential values I deeply held in my life, Curiosity stands at the top. I believe that there exists a harmonic balance between purpose and freedom. Having no sense of purpose would only make me feel lost, but hyper-focusing on my goal and not experiencing life to the fullest wouldn't be good either. Curiosity connects the two by constantly trying to learn new things and finding ways to connect them with my purpose. It makes me not a specialist, nor a generalist, but a superpositionist!
